The award-winning People & Planet Green League is the only league table ranking the environmental and ethical performance of all UK universities. It is produced annually by People & Planet, the UK's largest student network campaigning to end world poverty, defend human rights and protect the environment. The Green League is credited with driving forward the university sector's transition to a low-carbon future and is part of the Going Greener campaign which brings together students and staff to make their campuses fair and sustainable.
